
# House on Fire

**House on Fire** is a mobile-friendly platform designed to help users discover and rate house music parties across different cities. The app aims to provide all the essential details for party-goers and house music enthusiasts.

## Features

### Party Discovery
- Browse a curated collection of house music parties.
- Filter parties by city to find events near you or in places you plan to visit.

### Mobile Experience
- Fully optimized for mobile devices for easy access on the go.

### Ratings & Reviews
- View ratings for each party to see which events are most popular or highly recommended.
- Read and submit reviews to share your experience with others.

### Event Details
- See the start time for each party to plan your night.
- Get information on the artists performing at each event.
- Discover the specific sub-genres of house music featured (e.g., Afro House, Deep House, Tech House).

### Artist & Music Information
- Learn more about the DJs and artists playing at each party.
- Explore the styles of house music to find events that match your taste.

## Future Enhancements

- Add user profiles and personalized recommendations.
- Integrate ticket purchasing or RSVP functionality.
- Enable social sharing to invite friends to parties.
- Provide maps and directions to event venues.
